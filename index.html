FAQ

Is this official NBA or Top Shot?
No. This is an independent project built to read publicly available on-chain data from Flow.
How do my Moments stay safe?
We only read your collection. You never transfer custody. All ownership checks happen on-chain.
Can I play without Top Shot?
Yes. Demo decks are available. Real competitive play requires owned Moments.
When can I play?
Private playtests are ongoing. Public alpha launches Q2 2025.
Is it free to start?
Yes. No gas fees for matches. Ranked queues may have entry requirements.
